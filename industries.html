Hydrogen Retrofit Solutions for Industrial Energy Systems

Pazh Powertrain develops retrofit-oriented solutions for distributed power, cleaner combustion pathways, and modular upgrade strategies for legacy industrial equipment.

Hydrogen Retrofit Powertrain Platform

Pazh Powertrain develops a modular hydrogen retrofit platform designed to transition existing industrial power systems toward low-emission operation without requiring full asset replacement. Our technology focuses on practical integration with legacy propulsion and power generation systems, enabling operators to adopt cleaner combustion pathways while preserving reliability and infrastructure investment. The system has progressed through early-stage development, including core control architecture design and prototype validation. Current efforts are focused on system integration and preparing for real-world pilot deployment across targeted sectors. Explore how this platform is applied across aerospace, marine, rail, and industrial power systems.

Upgrade Instead of Replace

Rather than forcing a full asset replacement, Pazh’s concept focuses on retrofit pathways that can extend system value while improving sustainability and operational flexibility.

Industrial Decarbonization Pathway

The solution is intended for sectors where reliable thermal and power delivery remain critical, including remote, off-grid, and heavy-duty industrial environments.
