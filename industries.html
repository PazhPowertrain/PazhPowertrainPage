Industrial Applications

Hydrogen Retrofit Solutions for Industrial Energy Systems

Pazh Powertrain develops retrofit-oriented solutions for distributed power, cleaner combustion pathways, and modular upgrade strategies for legacy industrial equipment.

Aerospace

Pazh Powertrain develops hydrogen retrofit pathways for aerospace propulsion systems, focusing on turboshaft and specialized aviation applications where electrification is not practical.

The approach supports transition without full fleet replacement, enabling lower-emission operation while maintaining mission capability.

Marine

Marine transport requires high power and reliability. Our retrofit concept enables hydrogen-based transition strategies for vessels without requiring complete system replacement.

This is especially relevant for ports, tugboats, and heavy-duty maritime operations.

Rail

Hydrogen retrofit concepts offer a pathway for reducing emissions in rail systems while preserving high-value locomotive assets.

Pazh leverages control system and powertrain experience to support practical deployment in freight and industrial rail applications.

Industrial Power

Many industrial systems depend on legacy combustion assets. Our solution enables modular retrofit approaches for distributed and off-grid power systems.

The focus is on practical decarbonization while maintaining reliability and scalability.
