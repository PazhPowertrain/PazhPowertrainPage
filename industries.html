Industrial Applications

Hydrogen Retrofit Powertrain Platform

Pazh Powertrain develops a modular hydrogen retrofit platform designed to transition existing industrial power systems toward low-emission operation without requiring full asset replacement.

Our technology focuses on practical integration with legacy propulsion and power generation systems, enabling operators to adopt cleaner combustion pathways while preserving reliability and infrastructure investment.

The system has progressed through architecture development, control-layer definition, and prototype-oriented validation. Current efforts are focused on integration readiness and customer-facing deployment pathways.

Explore how this platform is applied across aerospace, marine, rail, and distributed power.

Upgrade Instead of Replace

Rather than forcing full asset replacement, Pazh focuses on retrofit pathways that preserve installed value while improving sustainability, flexibility, and long-term decarbonization readiness.

Sector-Focused Applications

The platform is intended for sectors where electrification is difficult and reliability remains critical, including aerospace, marine, rail, and distributed industrial power systems.

Built for Real-World Deployment

From modular hydrogen integration concepts to legacy powertrain adaptation, Pazh’s approach is built around practical engineering, staged validation, and commercial deployment readiness.
