Industrial Applications

Hydrogen Retrofit Solutions for Industrial Energy Systems

Pazh Powertrain develops retrofit-oriented solutions for distributed power, cleaner combustion pathways, and modular upgrade strategies for legacy industrial equipment.

Target Industries

Pazh Powertrain develops hydrogen retrofit solutions for hard-to-electrify sectors where legacy assets, thermal demand, and operational reliability remain critical. Our approach focuses on practical transition pathways that reduce emissions while preserving asset value.

Aerospace

The aerospace sector is one of the most difficult industries to decarbonize due to strict weight, safety, and performance requirements. Pazh Powertrain is developing retrofit-focused hydrogen combustion pathways for turboshaft and related propulsion systems, targeting practical transition solutions rather than full platform replacement.

This approach is especially relevant for helicopter and specialized aviation applications, where operators need lower-emission propulsion concepts while maintaining mission capability and fleet continuity.

  • Hydrogen retrofit pathway for legacy propulsion systems
  • Focus on hard-to-electrify aviation segments
  • Supports staged validation and commercialization

Marine

Marine transport is under growing pressure to reduce emissions, especially in ports, coastal operations, and heavy-duty working vessels. Pazh Powertrain’s retrofit strategy can support hydrogen transition concepts for marine powertrains where durability, power density, and fuel flexibility are essential.

Rather than requiring complete fleet replacement, our concept targets modular integration and upgrade pathways for vessels that need realistic decarbonization options within existing operating environments.

  • Relevant to tugboats, workboats, and heavy-duty marine vessels
  • Designed around retrofit practicality
  • Supports port decarbonization and low-emission operations

Rail

Rail systems require robust propulsion and power solutions capable of operating in demanding environments over long duty cycles. Hydrogen retrofit concepts offer a promising pathway for reducing emissions in locomotives and rail support equipment without forcing immediate replacement of high-value assets.

Pazh Powertrain’s background in control and powertrain integration supports the development of practical rail transition architectures for cleaner freight and industrial rail operations.

  • Applicable to retrofit-oriented locomotive concepts
  • Supports heavy-duty and long-duration operations
  • Builds on real-world control system integration experience

Industrial Power Generation

Many industrial and remote energy systems still depend on legacy combustion-based assets for reliable thermal and electrical power. Pazh Powertrain’s retrofit philosophy is well suited to distributed power and industrial equipment where operators need lower-emission upgrades without losing reliability.

Our approach supports modular deployment concepts for industrial decarbonization, particularly in off-grid, remote, and energy-intensive settings where a gradual transition is more realistic than immediate replacement.

  • Relevant to distributed and off-grid power systems
  • Upgrade instead of replace approach
  • Focused on real-world deployment and scalability
